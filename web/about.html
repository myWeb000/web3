Who We Are

Built on the belief that style has no limits

LUXE started as a small boutique in 2018 with a single idea: everyone deserves access to clothing that makes them feel confident, comfortable, and uniquely themselves.

From our first 50-item catalog to a curated collection of 12,000+ products across kids, men, and women — we've grown, but our mission hasn't changed. We source responsibly, design purposefully, and sell honestly.

Today, LUXE ships to 40+ countries, partners with over 50 independent designers, and is worn by over 1 million customers worldwide.
